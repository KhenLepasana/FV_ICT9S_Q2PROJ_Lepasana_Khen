Tortang Talong

Ingredients:

  • 4 pieces Chinese eggplant
  • 2 pieces raw eggs
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 6 tablespoons cooking oil

Procedure:

  1. The eggplant should be grilled until the skin is nearly black.
  2. After allowing the eggplant to cool, remove the skin, put aside.
  3. Crack the eggs over a bowl with salt then beat.
  4. Using a fork, flatten the eggplant on a level surface.
  5. Pour the beaten egg mixture over the flattened eggplant.
  6. Pour cooking oil into the heated pan.
  7. After dipping the eggplant in the beaten mixture, fry it. Ensure that the food is cooked on both sides on medium heat, fry for approximately 3 to 4 minutes on each side.

Beef Tapa with Sinangag

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb. beef sirloin sliced thinly
  • 3 pieces eggs
  • 6 tablespoons cooking oil
Tapa Marinade:
  • 3 tablespoons Knorr Liquid Seasoning
  • 6 cloves crushed garlic
  • ¾ cups pineapple juice
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • ¼ teaspoon ground white pepper
Sinangag:
  • 5 cups leftover rice
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 5 cloves garlic crushed

Procedure:

  1. In a bowl, combine the tapa marinade ingredients with the beef to marinate it. Refrigerate overnight with a lid on.
  2. Stir-fry the garlic in heated oil until it turns light brown, then cook the rice with garlic. Stir-fry for 5 to 8 minutes after adding the rice and seasoning with salt, put aside.
  3. In a pan, bring the marinated beef and water to a boil to prepare the tapa. Reduce the liquid, then add the oil and cook until it's medium brown, put aside.
  4. Sprinkle salt over the eggs and fry them until the yolks are cooked through.
  5. To make Tapsilog, plate the fried egg, garlic fried rice, and beef tapa. Serve with a dipping sauce of vinegar.
