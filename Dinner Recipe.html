Chicken Adobo

Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs chicken
  • 3 pieces dried bay leaves
  • 4 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 6 tablespoons white vinegar
  • 5 cloves garlic
  • 1 1/2 cups water
  • 3 tablespoons cooking oil
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon whole peppercorn

Procedure:

  1. Combine chicken, soy sauce, and garlic in a large bowl. Mix well. Marinate the chicken for at least 1 hour.
  2. Heat a cooking pot. Pour cooking oil and pan-fry the marinated chicken for 2 minutes per side.
  3. Pour in the remaining marinade, including garlic. Add water and bring to a boil.
  4. Add dried bay leaves and whole peppercorn. Simmer for 30 minutes or until the chicken is tender.
  5. Add vinegar. Stir and cook for 10 minutes.
  6. Put in the sugar and salt. Stir and turn the heat off. Serve hot. Enjoy!

Chicken Tinola

Ingredients:

  • 3 lbs chicken, cut into serving pieces
  • 2 green papaya, wedged
  • 1 ½ cup malunggay leaves
  • 1 cup hot pepper leaves
  • 5 cloves garlic, crushed and chopped
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 thumbs ginger, julienne
  • 2 Maggi Magic Chicken Cubes
  • 1 quart rice wash (see notes)
  • 1 quart water
  • 2 tablespoons fish sauce
  • Ground black pepper to taste (optional)
  • 3 tablespoons cooking oil

Procedure:

  1. Heat cooking oil in a cooking pot. Sauté garlic, onion, and ginger until the onion softens.
  2. Add the chicken. Continue sautéing for 2 minutes or until it turns light brown in color.
  3. Pour in 2 tablespoons of fish sauce. Stir.
  4. Add the rice wash into the cooking pot. Let it boil. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. Add water as needed. Let it boil.
  6. Put the green papaya wedges into the pot. Continue to simmer for 15 to 20 minutes.
  7. Add Maggi Magic Chicken Cubes. Stir. Season with ground black pepper (optional). Turn off the heat and then add the hot pepper leaves and malunggay leaves into the cooking pot. Cover for 2 minutes to let the residual heat cook the green veggies.
  8. Transfer to a serving bowl. Serve. Share and enjoy!
